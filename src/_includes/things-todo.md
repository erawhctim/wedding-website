**Do Something**
- Walk the beltline: Take a pit stop at Ponce City Market or Krog Street
- Piedmont Park: See why Atlanta is known as "The City in the forrest"
- The High Museum of Art: close to the Fox
- The Clermont Lounge: An Atlanta staple, but consider yourself warned
- Thrift shop in Little Five Points
- Oakland Cemetery: One of the most beautiful spots in Atlanta, and 0% spooky
- The Atlanta History Center: Beautiful museum if you like history
- Got more time? Drive a bit out of the city and take a fantastic hike
